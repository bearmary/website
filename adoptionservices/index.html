Adoption and Services

Waiting List

Joining our waiting list ensures that you will have priority in selecting a kitten with the specific characteristics you desire. By joining the waiting list, you can be assured of your satisfaction. Due to the restricted number of kittens available in each litter, we only have a limited number of spots on the waiting list.

The working process of the waiting list is:

  1. You need to deposit 500 CAD which is not refundable within 2 years. If, after this period, you have not chosen a desired kitten, a refund will be provided.
  2. Subsequently, you will be required to specify the preferred color and coat type (shorthair or longhair) for your desired kitten, like Ny shorthair, Ay longhair or golden point shorthair.
  3. Then your place in waiting list will be secured.
  4. At the age of two months, we will present the kittens to all parents in the queue based on their respective positions on the waiting list. Additionally, we will arrange a video phone call to discuss the details of the kitten. During this time, the adoption fee will be made known to all parents.
  5. If you decide to select a specific kitten, a deposit equivalent to “30% of the full adoption fee minus 500 CAD” will be required.
  6. In the event that you are unable to make a decision regarding the available kittens, you will be deferred to the next litter. It is important to note that your position on the waiting list may be moved forward.
  7. For customers residing outside of Canada, we offer multiple payment options, ensuring convenience and ease of transaction..

Available Cat Adoption Process

In various situations, there may occasionally be one or two adorable kittens eagerly awaiting their prospective owners. To showcase these adorable felines, we will share their photographs and videos on both our website and social media platforms.

The adoption process for an available cat entails the following steps:

  1. Express your interest by contacting us. We will schedule a video call to discuss the particulars of the kittens currently available for adoption.
  2. To secure your adoption, a deposit of 30% of the total adoption fee is required.

After choosing your kittens

After selecting your kittens, it is crucial to prioritize their hygiene and health. The following measures will be taken to ensure their well-being:

  1. Veterinary visits: We will schedule three veterinary appointments for your kittens, starting at 8 weeks of age and recurring every 4 weeks. During these visits, they will receive in totoal three doses FVRCP vaccination and body exam, which are already included in the adoption fee.
  2. Deworming: At 2 months of age, your kittens will undergo deworming treatment, which is also included in the adoption fee.
  3. Neuter/spay procedure: When your kittens reach 4 months of age, they will be scheduled for a neuter or spay surgery, which is included in the adoption fee.
  4. Rabies vaccination: Prior to leaving for their new home, your kittens will receive a rabies vaccination, ensuring their protection. This vaccination is also included in the adoption fee..
  5. Microchip option: Additionally, we offer the option to have your kittens microchipped. For an extra fee of 45 CAD (tax included).

By adhering to these hygiene and health practices, we aim to provide your kittens with a healthy start in their new home.

Methods of Transporting Cats to Their New Homes

  1. If you are located in close proximity to Toronto, Ottawa, or Montreal, we can deliver the kitten for free.
  2. For those living in other areas of Canada, we can assist with transporting your pet via cargo flight, with the cost of flight ranging from 200 to 300 CAD.
  3. If you reside in the eastern part of the United States, in close proximity to the Canadian border, and possess the ability to enter Canada, we can deliver the kittens to your desired location within Toronto, Ottawa, or Montreal for free.
  4. For individuals situated in Seatle, we can facilitate a flight to Vancouver. You have the option to either personally pick up your pet in Vancouver or allow us to assist you in finding a driver who can transport the pet across the border.
  5. For other cat lovers reside in other areas, there are alternative methods available, such as cargo transportation and flying with a nanny. For further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.

After arriving home

  1. It is highly recommended that you purchase pet insurance prior to the arrival of the kitten. This will ensure maximum protection for both you and the kitten.
  2. We will offer a comprehensive warranty for your kitten, covering illnesses such as feline plague, feline infectious peritonitis, and other specified conditions. The details of this warranty are outlined in our purchase agreement with you.
  3. We offer a lifetime of free consultations for the kitten, and we can provide you with medical assistance if needed through our veterinarians.
